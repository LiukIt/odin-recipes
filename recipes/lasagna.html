Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the holdest type, made of very wide, flat sheet.

Ingredients

  • Fresh lasagne sheets
  • Olive oil
  • Ragù
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water
  2. Prepare Ragù: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture toghether in a bowl
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly
