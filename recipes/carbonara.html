Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese, guanciale, and black pepper.

The dish took its modern form and name in the middle of the 20th century.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Thick pasta
  • Pecorino
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta: Put a pot of salted water on the burner
  2. Pour yolks: While pasta is cooking pour the yolks into a bowl and add pecorino cheese
  3. Add black pepper: Season with black pepper and whip by hand.
  4. Cook guanciale: When the guanciale will be cooked, drain the pasta dicrectly into the pan and stir it briefly to season it
  5. Add cooking water: To make your pasta very creamy, add a little cooking water to your pasta
