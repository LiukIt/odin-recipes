Pizza Margherita

Description

Pizza Margherita is a typical Neapolitan pizza, roundish in shape with a raised edge (the cornicione).

Is usually garnished with hand-crushed peeled tomatoes, mozzarella (buffalo mozzarella or fior di latte), fresh basil leaves, and extra virgin olive oil.

Ingredients

  • strong bread flour
  • instant yeast
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella
  • basil leaves

Steps

  1. Make the base: Put the flour into a large bowl, then salt. Pour in water and olive oil and bring together.
  2. Make the sauce: Mix the passata, basil and crushed garlic together, then season to taste.
  3. Roll out the dough: On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large rounds, about 25cm across, using a rolling pin.
  4. Top and bake: Bake. Then smooth sauce over bases with the back of a spoon. Scatter with cheese and tomatoes, drizzle with olive oil and season. Add basil leaves as a final touch.
